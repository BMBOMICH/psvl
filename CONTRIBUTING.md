# Contributing to PSVL

Thank you for helping improve PSVL.

## How to Contribute

- **Found a legal gap?** [Open an issue](../../issues/new?template=legal-gap-report.md) describing the missing protection
- **Want to add a clause?** [Submit a Pull Request](../../pulls) with the new clause
  and explain why it belongs in PSVL
- **Found a conflict between clauses?** [Open an issue](../../issues/new?template=clause-conflict-report.md) with the clause
  numbers and the conflict you identified
- **Spotted a typo or formatting issue?** [Submit a Pull Request](../../pulls)

## Important

- All contributions to this repository are subject to the
  intellectual property assignment terms in [Section 5 of PSVL](PSVL-1.0.txt)
- By submitting a Pull Request you confirm you are at least 18
  years old and agree to the contributor terms in [Section 5 of PSVL](PSVL-1.0.txt)
- The Author has sole discretion to accept or reject any contribution

## Credit

Accepted contributors will be credited in the [CHANGELOG](CHANGELOG.md).
