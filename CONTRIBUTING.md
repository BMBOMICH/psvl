# Contributing to PSVL

Thank you for helping improve PSVL.

## How to Contribute

- **Found a legal gap?** Open an issue describing the missing protection
- **Want to add a clause?** Submit a Pull Request with the new clause
  and explain why it belongs in PSVL
- **Found a conflict between clauses?** Open an issue with the clause
  numbers and the conflict you identified
- **Spotted a typo or formatting issue?** Submit a Pull Request

## Important

- All contributions to this repository are subject to the
  intellectual property assignment terms in Section 5 of PSVL
- By submitting a Pull Request you confirm you are at least 18
  years old and agree to the contributor terms in PSVL Section 5
- The Author has sole discretion to accept or reject any contribution

## Credit

Accepted contributors will be credited in the CHANGELOG.
