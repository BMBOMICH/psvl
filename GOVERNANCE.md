# PSVL Governance

## Model

PSVL follows a **single-maintainer (BDFL)** model.

The project is created, owned, and maintained by **Elxan Hüseynov**
(GitHub: [**@BMBOMICH**](https://github.com/BMBOMICH)).

## Decision Making

All final decisions about the PSVL template — including which clauses
are added, removed, or modified — rest with the maintainer.

Community input is welcomed via:

- [**Pull Requests**](https://github.com/BMBOMICH/psvl/pulls) for concrete changes
- [**Issues**](https://github.com/BMBOMICH/psvl/issues) for clause proposals, legal
  gaps, and clause conflicts
- [**Discussions**](https://github.com/BMBOMICH/psvl/discussions) for open-ended
  questions and ideas

## Contribution Acceptance

All contributions to the PSVL template are subject to the intellectual
property transfer terms in [**Section 5 of PSVL.txt**](PSVL.txt). By
submitting any contribution, you assign rights in that contribution to
the maintainer, in accordance with those terms.

The maintainer has sole discretion to accept, reject, modify, or
defer any contribution.

## Versioning

PSVL follows informal license versioning (two-number format, similar to
Apache 2.0, GPL 3.0, BUSL 1.1, and Elastic 2.0):

- **Major** (`2.0`): substantial restructuring or fundamentally new
  obligations.
- **Minor** (`1.1`, `1.2`): added clauses, expanded carve-outs, or
  significant clarifications. Typo fixes and minor formatting updates
  are also rolled into the next minor release rather than getting a
  separate patch number.

Each release is tagged in Git and noted in [**CHANGELOG.md**](CHANGELOG.md).

## Succession

If the maintainer becomes unable to continue maintaining PSVL, the
project may be transferred to a designated successor as provided in
[**clause 6.13 of PSVL.txt**](PSVL.txt). Until then, the canonical
version remains the version published in this repository.
